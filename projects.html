Outreach Program

I am in the process of starting a computer science outreach program for the 2025-2026 school year at my local high school. My high school has a robotics team so the two programs would complement each other.

My primary goal is to introduce students to coding, networks, and database systems, bridging the learning gap for an ‘intimidating’ major. This program aims to encourage more students to pursue computer science by providing exposure and guidance.

I would love to give back to my community and inspire more girls to pursue this field as it can be rather intimidating to really consider a male-dominated field at a young age. Tutoring at IUN has made me realize that there is a want in regard to learning about computer science; however, the learning curve is incredibly steep for those who have not been exposed to it.
